Welcome To My Final Project Page

This is my final project in my portfolio. My partner and I was really going for something real calm but at the same time really interesting.
What this code produces is a ship shifting game game where whoever is playing has five lives and the objective is to keep the ship intact. The game also tracks
your score and if, as a user, the score of 10,000 is reached then you have beat the game and your a legend. The game is played with two buttons, left arrow and right arrow.
The two buttons are used to shift left (technically up) and right (technically down)
Do you think you have what it takes to become a legend in this game.
